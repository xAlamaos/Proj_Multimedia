Camellia japonica



This is not the only species known simply as camellia, but it is definitely the most popular. There are an estimated 3000 varieties of this same species, and as many hybrids between this and other species of the same genus. It is the inspiring flower of the novel A Dama das Camélias, by Alexandre Dumas Jr.
