Aloe ciliaris



Aloe ciliaris, or common climbing aloe, is a fine, hardy, fast-growing succulent plant from South Africa. These plants grow very quickly, producing long, thin, messy stems that jut upwards, producing large bright orange-red flowers when they hit the sun.
