Hibiscus rosa-sinensis



Hibiscus rosa-sinensis, also known as hibiscus or student's grease (due to the mucilaginous effect leaves, being able to shine shoes), is a woody, fibrous shrub, up to 5 meters high, originating in tropical Asia and Hawaii, where it is considered the national flower.
